3D Asset Creation

The following sections discuss the process I followed for creating the 3D assets (the bike, the body of this terminal, etc.) used in the hideout. The process wasn't identical for every 3D asset, but it generally went as follows:

  • Modeling
  • UV Unwrapping
  • Texturing
  • Animating
  • Exporting

There were a few constraints on the asset creation process (and the project as a whole); namely time, my artistic abilities, and having to serve the entire project on demand for the web.

With those constraints in mind, I decided I would try to replicate the video game ULTRAKILL's assets. I felt I was capable of creating a similar style in a reasonable amount of time, and its smaller textures and 3D models made them a good fit for serving on demand.

I also just really like the grunge-y aesthetic and lower resolution textures and linear filtering that looks like pixel art wrapped on a 3D model.

I don't think I nailed the look on a technical level, and my artistic abilities don't compete with the artists that worked on ULTRAKILL, but I think it gives a similar vibe.
